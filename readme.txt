Local Startpage Dashboard

What this is:
A single-file, local start page you open in your browser.
No accounts or subscriptiosn.  You own it. No internet required. Your data stays on your device.

How to use:
1. Unzip the folder
2. Double-click index.html
3. Optional: set it as your browser home page

Data:
- All links and notes are stored locally in your browser
- Use the Export button to back up your data
